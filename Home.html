Welcome to Bending Backward Gymnastics.

Bending Backward Gymnastics is an all-girls gymnastics program focused on skill building for ages 4-17 catering to the Jewish community.

Director: Sara Jacobs

Sara has been doing gymnastics since she was a young child. She loves helping girls develop the skills and confidence through gymnastics to go on and achieve great things. Sara taught Gymnastics in High School (Elizabeth, NJ). Sara has been the Gymnastics instructor for Camp Splash in Fairlawn since 2008 and has been running Bending Backward Gymnastics since the Fall of 2008. Sara has a Masters in Education and has been involved in informal education for 15 years.

Coaches:

Every session maintains a high ratio of coaches to participants. Generally there is a one to six or seven ratio of coaches to girls. All of the coaches have years of gymnastics experience and share a passion for developing the girls' skills and confidence.
